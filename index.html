MULE Expedition Outfitters Open House

It's back! Our Spring MULE Open House is set for Sunday, April 23rd from 10am - 2pm!

We take over the whole parking lot so you can display your Overland vehicle builds, meet other like-minded people, and check out their builds. Plus we will have a vendor row again this Spring. Already on board are Overland Coffee, ARB USA, Bigfoot Winch Ropes,and Basecamp Adventure Rentals. These vendors come out so you can chat with them directly about their products and see how they have built their vehicles for Overland use. We offer special discounts all day AND each of our vendors provide free raffle prizes to be given out during the event.

Our special guest this Spring will be Andrew St. Pierre White and his team from 4xoverland.com. See their vehicles, chat them up about their world travels and learn what it's like to Overland for a living. We're excited to have them in our part of the world for this event.

Of course, in addition to vehicles, our retail store has plenty of cool displays for you to check out including our Tepui Rooftop Tent which you can go up in and get a feel for what it's like to camp in luxury.

As always, FREE hotdogs and sodas until we run out. Good vibes. Good people. Good times.

We hope you can join us!
